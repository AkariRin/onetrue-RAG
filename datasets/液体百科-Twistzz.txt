Russel "Twistzz" Van Dulken (born November 14, 1999) is a Canadian professional Counter-Strike 2 player and a former Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player for FaZe Clan. He is the most accomplished North American player, having won the second Intel Grand Slam with Liquid in 2019, and the PGL Major in Antwerp along with the fourth Intel Grand Slam with FaZe Clan in 2022 and 2023, respectively, among other S-Tier trophies.
