Robin "ropz" Kool (born December 22, 1999) is an Estonian professional Counter-Strike 2 player who currently plays for Team Vitality and a former Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player. ropz is among the most decorated CS:GO and CS2 players, having won over 20 S-Tier tournaments across his career, including two Major championships and two ESL Grand Slams.
ropz is regarded as one of the greatest riflers of all time. He has ranked in every HLTV Top 20 ranking since his debut, peaking at #3 in 2023.

First Estonian to attend, and win, a Major, doing so at PGL Major Kraków 2017 and PGL Major Antwerp 2022, respectively.
Second player to win 2 ESL Grand Slams, winning the first with FaZe Clan at ESL Pro League Season 17, and the second with Team Vitality at IEM Melbourne 2025.
