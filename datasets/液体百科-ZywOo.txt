Mathieu "ZywOo" Herbaut (born November 9, 2000) is a French professional Counter-Strike 2 player who plays for Team Vitality as the AWPer and a former professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player. He is widely considered as one of the best players in the world ever since his breakthrough in late 2018, being named as the best player of 2019, 2020 and 2023, and a runner-up for the best player of 2021 and 2022. In addition, he has won a record 27 HLTV MVP awards, two Major championships and an ESL Grand Slam trophy.
