Ilya "m0NESY" Osipov (born May 1, 2005) is a Russian professional Counter-Strike 2 player who plays for Team Falcons as the AWPer and a former Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player.
m0NESY was named Rookie of the Year in 2022, his professional debut, by HLTV and placed seventh in their annual player rankings. He was later ranked the fourth best player of 2023 and the second best of 2024. He is known for his explosive AWPing and swift flick shots.

Records
He is the first Bosnian to make it to HLTV's Top 20 Players of the Year Ranking.
Most appearances in HLTV's Top 20 Players of the Year ranking (9).
Tied with dev1ce.
Most consecutive appearances in HLTV's Top 20 Players of the Year ranking (9).
Most top 5 appearances in HLTV's Top 20 Players of the Year ranking (7).
Tied with donk for the highest achieved rating at a "Big Event" (1.70). He did so at ESL One: New York 2017.
Holds the record for kills in a map at a Major after scoring 52 against FlipSid3 Tactics at MLG Columbus 2016.
He is the first player to win multiple HLTV MVP awards in three consecutive years.
Previously held the record for kills in regulation at a big event or any match between top teams on LAN with 40 against Team Liquid at ECS Season 4 Finals. His record was later taken over by KRIMZ who dropped 44 against MIBR at ESL Pro League Season 10: Finals.
He is the 7th player (the previous 6 being Zeus, GuardiaN, olofmeister, s1mple, allu, and AdreN) to make a Major final with 2 different teams, doing so with FaZe Clan at ELEAGUE Major: Boston 2018 and G2 Esports at PGL Major Stockholm 2021.
