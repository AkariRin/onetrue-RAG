Danil "donk" Kryshkovets (born January 25, 2007) is a Russian professional Counter-Strike 2 player and a former Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player who plays for Team Spirit as an entry fragger. He is widely considered as one of the best riflers in the world ever since his breakthrough in late 2023, being named as the best player of 2024, his rookie year. In addition, he has won 10 HLTV MVP awards, including an MVP from a Major Championship.
